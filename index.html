Powerful 7.7-Magnitude Quake Strikes Myanmar, Shakes Bangkok

Bangkok, Thailand - A powerful 7.7-magnitude earthquake struck central Myanmar on Friday, triggering panic in Bangkok as buildings shook and hundreds fled into the streets.

The U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) reported the earthquake. Germany's GFZ center for geosciences said the midday temblor was a shallow 10 kilometers. A strong 7.3 magnitude earthquake rocked the Thai capital Friday, causing buildings to sway.

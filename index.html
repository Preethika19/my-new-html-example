Powerful Earthquake Shakes Myanmar and Bangkok

Bangkok, Thailand - A strong 7.7-magnitude earthquake struck central Myanmar on Friday, causing buildings to sway in Bangkok and triggering panic among residents. The quake, which occurred around midday, sent shockwaves through the Thai capital, prompting evacuations of numerous high-rise buildings.
